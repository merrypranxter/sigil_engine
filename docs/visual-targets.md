# Visual Targets: sigil_engine

The sigil engine aims to capture the aesthetics of chaos magick while remaining computationally simple.

- **Condensed glyphs:** Letters are simplified into straight-line strokes and overlaid to create complex shapes.
- **Randomized overlay:** Each glyph is rotated and offset slightly to avoid symmetry and encourage organic forms.
- **Flame trails:** When charging, particles spawn along the strokes and drift upward, fading from bright orange to transparent black.
- **Particle burst:** At peak charge, a burst of particles emanates from the center, scattering outward.
- **Strobing:** A periodic inversion of the canvas colours creates a gnosis-like flicker effect.
- **Minimal UI:** The focus remains on the sigil; controls are unobtrusive.

These targets ensure the sigil remains central while the charging effects add dynamism and ritual atmosphere.
