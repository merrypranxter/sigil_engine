# sigil_engine

> Turn your intention into a generative chaos-magick sigil. Provide a phrase; vowels and duplicate letters are removed, leaving unique consonants which become glyphs. Letters are overlaid to form a unique seal, then charged through visual gnosis with flame trails, particle bursts, and strobing animations.

**Archetype:** GPGPU_GRID_SIM. Distinct from `runic_galdr` (runes and resonance) and `pulfrich_effect` (perception experiments).

## Adjacency
- `runic_galdr` — both projects translate symbolic systems into generative visuals.
- `particle_systems` — uses simple physics to create flame and burst effects.
- `shader_feedback` — ping-pong FBOs could be used for afterimages and strobing.

## Visual Targets
- Condensed letters drawn with straight line segments.
- Glyph overlay forming a unique seal; slight random rotations and offsets to break symmetry.
- Combustion animation: flickering flame trails along the strokes.
- Particle burst when the sigil is fully charged.
- Strobing inversion to evoke visual gnosis.

## Technical Notes
- Data: Each letter uses line segments in normalized 0–1 coordinates.
- Condensing: remove vowels (A,E,I,O,U) and duplicate letters; keep order.
- Charging: simple particle system spawns along strokes; fire-like colours fade with time; strobe toggling flips colours at random intervals.
